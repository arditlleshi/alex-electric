# Alex Electric

A modern, SEO-focused business website for an electrical services company in Albania.

The project was built with **Next.js**, **React**, **TypeScript**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **Bun**, with a strong focus on local SEO, responsive design, performance, and clear customer conversion flows.

Live website: https://alex-electric.com

---

## Overview

**Alex Electric** is a business website for electrical services in Albania, mainly focused on Tirana and Durrës.

The goal of the project is to help customers quickly understand the services offered, contact the business, and find location-specific service pages through search engines.

The website is designed to be simple, fast, mobile-friendly, and easy to use for people looking for professional electrical services.

---

## Main Services Covered

The website includes content and pages for:

- Electrical repairs
- Emergency electrician services
- Electrical installations
- EV charger installation
- Solar panel services
- Smart home services
- Electrical services for apartments
- Electrical services for villas
- Electrical services for businesses
- Local services in Tirana and Durrës
